🧠 THE THREE AGENTS

CONSERVATOR Risk-Averse
Waits for sustained critical issues. Acts only at 95%+ disk for 5+ minutes. High confidence in waiting.
PREVENTER Proactive
Prevents problems before they occur. Monitors velocity. Early intervention at 80%+.
INVESTIGATOR Analytical
Finds patterns and root causes. Detects anomalies. Conservative when uncertain.

Consensus: Agents vote with confidence-weighted scores. 50%+ agreement required for action.

Aegis learns from your behavior:

  • Actions succeed → Agent accuracy ↑
  • Actions fail → Agent accuracy ↓
